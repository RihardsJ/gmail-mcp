# Email Templates

A collection of 11 essential email templates for managing everyday personal tasks, from scheduling appointments to handling home maintenance and personal correspondence.

---

### 1. Responding to an Appointment Reminder

**Use Case:** When you receive an automated or manual reminder for an upcoming appointment (e.g., dentist, doctor, haircut) and you need to confirm your attendance.

**Subject:** Re: Confirming Appointment on [Date] at [Time]

Hi [Name/Office Name],

Thank you for the reminder.

This is to confirm that I will be attending my appointment scheduled for **[Date]** at **[Time]**.

I look forward to seeing you then.

Best regards,

Rihards J

### 2. Responding to a Quote for Home Maintenance

**Use Case:** After a tradesperson (plumber, electrician, etc.) has sent you a quote for a job and you want to proceed or ask a question.

**Subject:** Re: Quote for [Service Type, e.g., Leaky Faucet Repair] - [Your Address]

Hi [Tradesperson's Name/Company Name],

Thank you for sending over the quote for the [Service Type] at my home.

**[Option A: Accept Quote]**
The quote of [Amount] is acceptable. Please let me know your availability to schedule the work. I am generally free [Provide your availability, e.g., on weekday afternoons after 3 PM].

**[Option B: Ask a Question]**
The quote looks good, but I have one quick question. Does the price of [Amount] include [Specific item, e.g., the cost of all parts and materials]? Once confirmed, I'd like to schedule the work.

Thank you,

Rihards J
rihards.jukna@gmail.com

### 4. Responding to a Neighbor's Email (e.g., about noise, a package)

**Use Case:** A polite and cooperative response to a neighbor who has emailed you about a common issue.

**Subject:** Re: [Original Subject, e.g., Package Delivery / A quick question]

Hi [Neighbor's Name],

Thanks for the email.

**[Option A: For a Complaint, e.g., Noise]**
I'm so sorry to hear that my [source of noise, e.g., music] was disturbing you last night. I wasn't aware of how loud it was, and I'll be sure to keep it down in the future. Please don't hesitate to let me know if it happens again.

**[Option B: For a Favor/Info, e.g., Package]**
Thanks for letting me know my package arrived! I'll be home around [Time] to pick it up from you. I really appreciate you holding onto it for me.

Thanks again,

Rihards J
rihards.jukna@gmail.com

### 7. Responding to a Bill or Invoice You Have a Question About

**Use Case:** When you receive a bill (utility, medical, etc.) that is higher than expected or contains a charge you don't understand.

**Subject:** Question Regarding Invoice #[Invoice Number]

Dear [Company Name/Billing Department],

I am writing in regard to invoice #[Invoice Number], dated [Date], for my account, #[Account Number].

I have a question about a specific charge on the bill. Could you please provide more details on the line item listed as "[Name of Charge]" for the amount of [Amount]?

I have attached a copy of the invoice for your reference. I would appreciate your clarification before I submit my payment.

Thank you for your assistance.

Sincerely,

Rihards J
rihards.jukna@gmail.com

### 8. Responding to a Follow-Up from a Service Provider

**Subject:** Re: Following up on your [Service/Product]

Hi [Name],

Thank you for following up with me.

**[Option A: If you're ready to proceed]**
Yes, I have had a chance to review the information. I would like to proceed with [The action, e.g., scheduling the car repair]. Please let me know the next steps.

**[Option B: If you need more time]**
I appreciate the reminder. I haven't had a chance to make a final decision yet, but I plan to review everything this weekend. I will get back to you by [Date, e.g., Monday].

Thanks,

Rihards J

I'm writing to quickly confirm that the shipping address is correct.
